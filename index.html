Hypertrophy Micro-Workout Program

Designed for desk workers: 5–10 minute sessions every hour

How It Works

  • Do one mini workout every hour (up to 10x/day)
  • Each session: 1–2 exercises, 2–3 sets close to failure
  • Minimal equipment: bodyweight, dumbbells, bands
  • Split by movement type to ensure recovery and coverage
  • Designed to be incorporated in to your workday to break up sedentary day

Backed by research: Schoenfeld et al. (2021) on training frequency and hypertrophy, and Oikawa et al. (2019) on short-duration hypertrophy training.

Sunday – Optional Recovery

  • Mobility drills (shoulder circles, hip openers, thoracic twists)
  • Foam rolling or light yoga
  • Walking or zone 2 cardio for 20–30 min

Progression Tips

  • Increase reps over time (progressive overload)
  • Add resistance with bands, dumbbells, or tempo (slow eccentrics)
  • Alternate harder variations weekly (e.g., decline push-ups, split squats)
  • Track volume: aim for 10–20 challenging sets per muscle group/week
